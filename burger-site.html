Burgers Built Different.

SMASH started in 2021 with one griddle, one recipe, and one obsession — making the best smash burger in the city. No shortcuts. No freezers. Just fresh beef, real ingredients, and a whole lot of heat.

Every patty is hand-balled and pressed to order. That's the smash difference — a crispy lacey crust on the outside, juicy and tender on the inside. Once you taste it, there's no going back.
